About Delaney

Delaney Phelps is a Carolina girl from birth. Raised throughout the South, she spent most of her youth in Asheville, North Carolina, where her family still lives. She’s been writing since she was eight years old, and she currently studies creative writing at UNC Chapel Hill. She’s been awarded the Mini-Max Award for Flash Fiction and the George B. Wynne Award during her time at UNC. She hopes to enter an MFA program and continue to write after graduation.
